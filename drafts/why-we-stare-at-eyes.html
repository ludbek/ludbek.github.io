Why We Stare At Eyes

PUBLISHED ON March 05, 2015
THOUGHTS ON eye pyschology

When we talk, see or watch someone we often stare them at their eyes. I believe there are three reasons for this.

  1. their color distinction is dominant compared to other parts of the face

  2. eyes have movement

  3. focusing at an eye our brain can get symmetry of entire face
